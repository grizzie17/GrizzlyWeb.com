Grizzly Web Links (Home) >> Submit Link
 
The Grizzly Web Links is a web directory.  The directory is hierarchically organized according to subject -- from broad to specific.  The Grizzly Web Links is maintained by real people who evaluate sites for inclusion in the directory.  Each submission is subject to individual evaluation and review.  We aren't a search engine and we pride ourselves on being highly selective.  We don't accept all sites, so please don't take it personally should your site not be accepted. Submitting a site is easy, but we ask that you take a few moments to understand how to submit a site before you begin. There are few simple steps. 
  1. Please read and understand our Submission Policies before you submit your site. (See box to the right) 
  2. Do a quick search in the directory to be sure your site isn't already listed. This saves everyone time. 
  3. Identify the single best category for your site.
    • The Grizzly Web Links has a large set of subjects to choose from, and by submitting your site to the most specific category/subject, you help insure that your site will be reviewed by the correct person, thus giving your site a better chance of acceptance. 
    • Once you've selected the best category for your site, go directly to that category and then click "Add URL." 
We welcome your comments and feedback.

Submission Policies

We care a great deal about the quality of the Grizzly Web Links directory. Please be aware that abusive submissions will result in the removal of all sites connected with the abuser. 
  • You must specify a valid email address.  Any submission that does not have a valid and reachable email address will not be considered for addition to the directory.  The specified email address is used for site maintenance and will not be provided to any external resources.
  • A valid and understandable description is necessary.  Our site evaluation process starts with reading your description.  If the description is unintelligible the site will not be evaluated.  If the description does not match your real site, the submission will be rejected.
  • Please only submit an URL to the Grizzly Web Links once. If a site has not been listed within four weeks, you may submit it again.
  • Do not submit multiple individual pages.  Multiple page submissions will be considered as submission abuse and result in the removal of all sites associated with the abuser.
  • Submit sites to the most relevant category/page. Sites intentionally submitted to inappropriate or unrelated categories will not be added.
  • Disguising your submission and submitting the same URL more than once is not permitted.
    Example: http://grizzlyweb.com and http://www.grizzlyweb.com/
  • Please do not submit mirror sites. Mirror sites are sites that contain identical content, but have different URLs altogether. 
  • Please avoid submitting sites with addresses that redirect to another address.
  • Sites with illegal, adult, or licentious content are forbidden in the Grizzly Web Links. Examples of illegal material include, but are not limited to, child pornography and sites infringing on copyright.  Examples of adult material include, sex pictures, sex chat rooms, or gambling and casinos.
  • Wait until your site is complete before submitting your URL. Sites which are incomplete, contain "Under Construction" notices, or contain broken graphics or links aren't good candidates for the directory.
  • Sites consisting largely of affiliate links should not be submitted.
  • At this point non-english sites are not accepted, but we should be allowing them in the future.
  • Unfortunately we will refuse sites from certain domains, due to historic massive spamming from those domains.
